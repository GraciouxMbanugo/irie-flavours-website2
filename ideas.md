## Selected Design: **Option A — "Roots & Riddim"**

This approach best captures the authentic Jamaican cultural identity the client wants to showcase. The earthy Rastafarian palette (black, gold, green) directly references the Jamaican flag and cultural heritage. The diagonal cuts and asymmetric layouts create visual dynamism that mirrors the energy of Jamaican music and street food culture. The hand-crafted aesthetic feels genuine rather than corporate, which is exactly right for a family-style Caribbean kitchen.
